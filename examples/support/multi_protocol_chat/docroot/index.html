Websocket Echo Demo

This demo simply echoes your messages to all connected websocket clients.

To connect through telnet ...

Input the following upgrade request and press enter a couple of times. You'll get a 100 Continue response and see the websocket client count at the right increments up. Messages are now shared between your console and this websocket application! 

$ telnet 174.107.156.0 80

GET /line-feed-chat HTTP/1.1
Host: 174.107.156.0
Connection: Upgrade
Upgrade: line-feed-chat
